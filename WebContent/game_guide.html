Welcome to Fantasy League Classic 2016/17. Fantasy League Classic returns this summer with the start of another Premier League season on Saturday, 13 August!

Compete against managers from far and wide and win big with over £50,000 in prizes across a wide range of competitions.

The game covers the entire 2016/17 Premier League campaign, with competitions for overall, monthly and weekly standings as well as plenty of side games meaning managers will always have something to play for!

Select your squad and follow your team's progress as your players score points for goals, assists and clean sheets.

Use our automatic Supersubs tool to select the players you want to play, and make those all-important transfers to get your team back on the right track if and when things go awry.

Visit the Squad Selection page to get started.

You're in charge!

As a Fantasy League Classic manager, you'll pick your squad of 16 Premier League stars from a budget of £75 million.

All players and prices are listed on our Squad Selection page, so send your scouts off in that direction.

You can choose from the best talent the Premier League has to offer, fine tuning your squad while keeping to our club quota, formation and budget rules.

Tinker with your team using unlimited transfers until 12:30 BST on Saturday, 13 August, then use Supersubs and transfers throughout the season to maximise your points potential.

Visit the Squad Selection & Changes page for more information.

Your team will score points based upon how your chosen players perform in Barclays Premier League matches (or FA Cup matches for the FA Cup competition) during the 2016/17 season.

Score points for goals, assists and clean sheets, while minimising goals against for your keeper and defenders. Silver and Gold package subscribers even get live points updates as the matches happen!

Visit the Scoring Points page for more information.

Fantasy League Classic offers the widest array of competitions, with weekly and monthly leaderboards in addition to the season-long competition. You'll also have the chance to do battle in our FA Cup, Double Team and Gold & Silver competitions. So even if your initial signings fail to deliver, there is always the opportunity to turn your team around and win great prizes!

Embark on a quest for personal glory in our Premier League competition, which places your team in a league of 20, where you’ll compete for qualification into tiered groups. Then battle it out in Champions League style group stages, knockouts and - for the survivors - a final week sprint to decide the ultimate honours.

Keep your friends close and your enemies closer by setting up or joining your own Friends & Enemies League to keep the banter going throughout the season!

Visit the Competitions & Leagues page for more information.

Fantasy League Classic offers you the chance to win over £50,000 worth of prizes!

Not only is there a fabulous first prize of £10,000 in cash, but there's also plenty more to be won with over 120 different cash prizes, as well as those much sought after Fantasy League medals!

Visit the Prizes page for more information.
